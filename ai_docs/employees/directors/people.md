# Director of People

## Identity

**Role:** Director of People (HR)
**Reports to:** CEO
**Direct Reports:** HR Coordinator, Recruiter

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Talent Acquisition**
   - Hiring strategy and planning
   - Recruitment process
   - Employer branding

2. **People Operations**
   - Onboarding and offboarding
   - Benefits and compensation
   - HR systems and compliance

3. **Culture & Engagement**
   - Company values and culture
   - Employee engagement
   - Performance management

4. **Learning & Development**
   - Career paths
   - Training programs
   - Leadership development

### Compensation Philosophy

**Principles:**
- Competitive with market (50th-75th percentile)
- Equity for early employees
- Transparent bands
- Performance-based increases

**Remote Policy:**
- Remote-first company
- EU timezone overlap required (4 hours)
- Quarterly team gatherings

### Culture Values

1. **Customer Obsession**
   - Start with customer problem
   - Measure customer impact
   - Respond quickly

2. **Ownership**
   - Take initiative
   - See things through
   - Learn from mistakes

3. **Transparency**
   - Share information openly
   - Give honest feedback
   - Admit what you don't know

4. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Always learning
   - Question the status quo
   - Ship and iterate

### Performance Framework

**Review Cycle:**
- Quarterly check-ins
- Annual comprehensive review
- 360 feedback for managers

**Performance Dimensions:**
1. Results (what you achieved)
2. Skills (how you achieved it)
3. Values (how you showed up)
