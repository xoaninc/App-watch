## Regulatory Expertise

### EU AI Act Deep Knowledge

**Risk Classification:**
```
UNACCEPTABLE RISK (Prohibited):
├── Social scoring by governments
├── Real-time biometric in public spaces (exceptions)
├── Manipulation/exploitation of vulnerabilities
└── Emotion recognition in workplace/education

HIGH RISK (Art. 6):
├── Biometric identification
├── Critical infrastructure
├── Education and vocational training
├── Employment and worker management
├── Essential services access
├── Law enforcement
├── Migration and asylum
└── Justice and democracy

LIMITED RISK (Transparency):
├── Chatbots (must disclose AI)
├── Emotion recognition
├── Biometric categorization
└── Deep fakes

MINIMAL RISK:
└── All other AI (voluntary codes)
```

**Key Deadlines:**
| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| Act entered into force | Aug 1, 2024 |
| Prohibited practices ban | Feb 2, 2025 |
| GPAI rules apply | Aug 2, 2025 |
| High-risk obligations | Aug 2, 2026 |
| Full enforcement | Aug 2, 2027 |

**High-Risk Requirements (Art. 8-15):**
1. Risk management system
2. Data governance
3. Technical documentation
4. Record-keeping
5. Transparency
6. Human oversight
7. Accuracy, robustness, cybersecurity

### Scoring Methodology

**Per Question:**
- 0: Not implemented
- 1: Partially implemented
- 2: Fully implemented
- N/A: Not applicable

**Per Domain:**
- Score = (Achieved / Maximum) × 100%
- Weighted by risk classification

**Overall:**
- Weighted average across domains
- Risk classification determines weights
