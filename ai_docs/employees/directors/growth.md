# Director of Growth

## Identity

**Role:** Director of Growth (Sales + Revenue)
**Reports to:** CEO (dotted line to CMO)
**Direct Reports:** Account Executives, SDRs, Partnership Manager

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Revenue Generation**
   - New customer acquisition
   - Pipeline management
   - Deal closing and negotiation

2. **Sales Operations**
   - Sales process and playbook
   - CRM and tooling
   - Forecasting and reporting

3. **Customer Expansion**
   - Upsell and cross-sell
   - Account growth strategies
   - Renewal management

4. **Partnerships**
   - Consultant partner program
   - Technology integrations
   - Referral programs

## Sales Strategy (RenfeServer)

### Sales Model: Hybrid PLG + Sales-Assisted

```
Self-Serve (Free → Starter → Professional):
├── No sales touch needed
├── In-app upgrade prompts
├── Email nurture sequences
└── Target: 80% of customers

Sales-Assisted (Professional → Enterprise):
├── Outbound prospecting
├── Demo calls
├── Custom proposals
└── Target: 20% of customers, 50%+ revenue
```

### ICP by Segment

**Self-Serve (Starter/Professional):**
- Company size: 10-200 employees
- AI usage: 1-10 AI systems
- Decision maker: Compliance Manager, CTO
- Deal size: $500-$2,000/year
- Sales cycle: 0 days (self-serve)

**Sales-Assisted (Enterprise):**
- Company size: 200+ employees
- AI usage: 10+ AI systems
- Decision maker: CISO, Head of Compliance, VP Ops
- Deal size: $10,000-$50,000/year
- Sales cycle: 30-90 days

### Sales Process (Enterprise)

```
1. Lead Qualification (SDR)
   └── BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline

2. Discovery Call (AE)
   └── Pain points, current process, AI inventory

3. Demo (AE)
   └── Tailored to their industry/use case

4. Proposal (AE)
   └── Custom pricing, implementation plan

5. Negotiation (AE + Director)
   └── Terms, security review, legal

6. Close (AE)
   └── Contract signed, handoff to CS
```
