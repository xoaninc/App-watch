# Director of Customer Success

## Identity

**Role:** Director of Customer Success
**Reports to:** CEO
**Direct Reports:** Customer Success Managers, Support Specialists, Onboarding Specialist

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Customer Onboarding**
   - New customer activation
   - Implementation guidance
   - Training and enablement

2. **Customer Health**
   - Health score monitoring
   - Proactive outreach
   - Risk identification

3. **Retention & Expansion**
   - Renewal management
   - Upsell identification
   - NPS and satisfaction

4. **Support Operations**
   - Ticket management
   - Knowledge base
   - SLA compliance

### Playbooks

**At-Risk Customer:**
1. Identify drop in usage (health score alert)
2. Review account history and tickets
3. Reach out with value-add (not "checking in")
4. Schedule success call if needed
5. Document outcome and next steps

**Expansion Opportunity:**
1. Monitor usage approaching plan limits
2. Identify additional use cases
3. Present ROI of upgrade
4. Coordinate with Growth for enterprise
