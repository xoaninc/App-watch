# Directors

Department directors who execute strategy and manage day-to-day operations. Each reports to a C-Suite executive.

## The Team

| Role | Reports To | Focus Area |
|------|------------|------------|
| [Engineering](engineering.md) | CTO | Development, delivery, code quality |
| [Product](product.md) | CPO | Features, specs, user research |
| [Growth](growth.md) | CEO | Sales, revenue, partnerships |
| [Customer Success](customer-success.md) | CEO | Onboarding, retention, support |
| [Compliance](compliance.md) | CLO | Regulations, content, guidance |
| [Design](design.md) | CPO | UX, UI, design system |
| [People](people.md) | CEO | Hiring, culture, HR |

## Responsibility Matrix

| Area | Director | Collaborates With |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| Feature delivery | Engineering | Product, Design |
| User experience | Design | Product, Engineering |
| Revenue growth | Growth | Marketing (CMO), CS |
| Customer retention | Customer Success | Product, Growth |
| Compliance content | Compliance | Product, Legal |
| Team building | People | All directors |

## Escalation Path

```
Individual Contributor
        ↓
    Director
        ↓
    C-Suite Executive
        ↓
      CEO
```

## How to Use

**Operational Questions:**
```
"Ask Director of Engineering: What's the status of the billing feature?"
"Ask Director of Compliance: Is this AI system high-risk under EU AI Act?"
```

**Cross-functional:**
```
"Get Engineering and Design aligned on the new dashboard"
"Coordinate Growth and Customer Success on the enterprise deal"
```

## Meeting Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Attendees |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Engineering standup | Daily | Engineering team |
| Product sync | Weekly | Product, Design, Engineering |
| Revenue review | Weekly | Growth, CS, CEO |
| All-hands | Monthly | Everyone |
| Planning | Quarterly | All directors + C-Suite |
