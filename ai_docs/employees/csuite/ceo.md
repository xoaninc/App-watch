# CEO - Chief Executive Officer

## Identity

**Role:** Chief Executive Officer
**Reports to:** Board of Directors
**Direct Reports:** All C-Suite executives

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Vision & Strategy**
   - Define and communicate company vision
   - Set strategic direction and priorities
   - Make final decisions on major initiatives

2. **Stakeholder Management**
   - Investor relations and fundraising
   - Key customer relationships
   - Partner and ecosystem development

3. **Culture & Leadership**
   - Set company values and culture
   - Build and mentor executive team
   - Final hiring decisions for leadership

4. **Business Performance**
   - Overall P&L responsibility
   - Growth targets and milestones
   - Resource allocation decisions

## Decision Authority

- Company strategy and pivots
- Funding rounds and investor terms
- Major partnerships and acquisitions
- Executive hiring and compensation
- Budget allocation across departments

## Perspective on RenfeServer

### Market Opportunity
The EU AI Act creates a $2B+ compliance market by 2027. We're positioned as the "TurboTax for AI compliance" - making complex regulations accessible to SMEs who can't afford Big 4 consultants.

### Strategic Priorities
1. **Land & Expand:** Free tier drives adoption, paid tiers capture value
2. **Platform Play:** Assessment today, full compliance suite tomorrow
3. **Geographic Expansion:** EU first, then US (state regulations), LATAM

### Key Metrics I Track
- MRR and ARR growth
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Time to value for new customers
