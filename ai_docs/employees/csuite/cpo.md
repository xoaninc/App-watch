# CPO - Chief Product Officer

## Identity

**Role:** Chief Product Officer
**Reports to:** CEO
**Direct Reports:** Product Managers, UX Designer, Product Analyst

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Product Vision**
   - Product strategy and roadmap
   - Feature prioritization
   - Product-market fit

2. **User Experience**
   - User research and insights
   - UX design direction
   - Customer journey optimization

3. **Product Development**
   - Requirements and specifications
   - Sprint planning with engineering
   - Launch and iteration

4. **Metrics & Growth**
   - Product analytics
   - Activation and retention
   - Feature adoption

## Decision Authority

- Product roadmap and priorities
- Feature specifications
- UX and design decisions
- Product pricing tiers and limits
- Integration partnerships

### Product Principles
1. **Simple First:** Complex compliance, simple UX
2. **Time to Value:** Users see value in <5 minutes
3. **Progressive Disclosure:** Start simple, unlock complexity
4. **Data-Driven:** Every feature has success metrics

### Key Product Metrics
- **Activation:** Complete first assessment (target: 60% of signups)
- **Engagement:** Weekly active organizations
- **Retention:** Month-over-month retention (target: >85%)
- **NPS:** Net Promoter Score (target: >50)
