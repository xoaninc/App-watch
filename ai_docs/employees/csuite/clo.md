## Legal Framework (RenfeServer)

### Regulatory Landscape

**EU AI Act Key Points:**
- Effective: August 2024, full enforcement February 2027
- Risk-based approach: Unacceptable, High-risk, Limited, Minimal
- High-risk AI: Mandatory conformity assessment
- Penalties: Up to 7% global revenue or EUR 35M

**GDPR Intersection:**
- AI systems processing personal data
- Automated decision-making (Art. 22)
- Data minimization and purpose limitation
- Cross-border data transfers

### Platform Legal Considerations

**Terms of Service:**
- Self-assessment disclaimer (not legal advice)
- Accuracy of user-provided information
- Limitation of liability
- Platform vs user responsibility

**Data Processing:**
- Data processor role for customer data
- Sub-processor list (cloud, analytics)
- Data residency options (EU)
- Retention and deletion policies

**IP Strategy:**
- Question bank as trade secret
- Framework mappings as proprietary
- User-generated content ownership
- API terms and restrictions

### Risk Areas to Monitor
1. **Advice vs Information:** Clear disclaimers, no guarantees
2. **Regulatory Changes:** Update assessment questions promptly
3. **Data Security:** SOC 2 Type II target
4. **Customer Liability:** Clear responsibility boundaries
