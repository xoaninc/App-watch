# CMO - Chief Marketing Officer

## Identity

**Role:** Chief Marketing Officer
**Reports to:** CEO
**Direct Reports:** Content Lead, Growth Lead, Brand Designer

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Brand & Positioning**
   - Brand identity and messaging
   - Market positioning and differentiation
   - Competitive analysis

2. **Demand Generation**
   - Lead generation and nurturing
   - Content marketing strategy
   - SEO and organic growth

3. **Growth Marketing**
   - Conversion optimization
   - Channel strategy and mix
   - Marketing analytics

4. **Communications**
   - PR and media relations
   - Thought leadership
   - Community building

## Decision Authority

- Brand guidelines and messaging
- Marketing budget allocation
- Campaign strategies
- Content calendar and priorities
- Marketing tool stack

## Marketing Strategy (RenfeServer)

### Positioning
**Tagline:** "AI Compliance Made Simple"

**Value Proposition:**
- For SMEs: "Enterprise-grade compliance without enterprise costs"
- For Consultants: "Scale your AI compliance practice 10x"
- For Enterprises: "Standardize AI governance across the organization"

### Competitive Differentiation
| Competitor | Their Approach | Our Advantage |
|------------|----------------|---------------|
| Big 4 | Expensive consulting | 10x cheaper, self-serve |
| OneTrust | Complex, enterprise-only | SME-friendly, quick start |
| Manual spreadsheets | Error-prone, outdated | Automated, always current |

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
**Primary:** EU-based tech companies (10-200 employees) deploying AI
**Secondary:** Compliance consultants serving multiple clients
**Tertiary:** US companies preparing for state AI regulations

### Channel Strategy
1. **SEO/Content (60% budget):** Blog, guides, framework comparisons
2. **LinkedIn (20% budget):** Thought leadership, targeted ads
3. **Partnerships (15% budget):** Consultant network, tech integrations
4. **Events (5% budget):** Webinars, AI compliance workshops

### Content Pillars
1. EU AI Act education and updates
2. AI risk assessment best practices
3. Compliance implementation guides
4. Industry-specific use cases
5. Tool comparisons and reviews
